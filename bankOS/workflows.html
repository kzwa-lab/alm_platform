Calculation Formulas

EVE = PV(Assets) − PV(Liabilities)
PV = Σ CFt / (1 + shocked_ratet)t
ΔEVE = EVEshocked − EVEbase
%T1 = |ΔEVE| / Tier1_Capital × 100
SOT Breach: ΔEVE > 15% of T1

DGAP = DA − k · DL
ΔEVE = −DGAP · MVA · (Δy/(1+y))
ΔP/P = −Dmod·Δy + ½·Convexity·Δy2

NIIt = Avg_Assets × Yieldt − Avg_Liabs × Costt + Hedge_PnLt
